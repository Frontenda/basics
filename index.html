Composer
PURPOSE
Teaching A.I. to design websites we are proud of.
BENEFIT
Custom sites on a scale not possible without help from A.I.
01
The Tuner
PURPOSE
To tune our layout, typography and color dials until we exceed site owners’ expectations.
BENEFIT
Allows site owner’s to give feedback on A.I. design decisions with language that doesn’t require a design degree.
02
The Mixer
PURPOSE
To choose a composition or a mixture of compositions based on Tuner.
BENEFIT
Allows us to offer a dizzying variety of layouts and styles by assigning each item to whichever composition is most appropriate and pleasing to the site owner.
03
The Tagger
PURPOSE
To tag sites and content based on site purpose, text length, image size, context etc.
BENEFIT
Enables the A.I. to make appropriate design decisions based on the content’s classification tags.
04
The Poet
PURPOSE
To define the flow of content and the area items will occupy based on tags and site purpose.
BENEFIT
Maximizes the impact of important items in the layout in relation to other items (related and unrelated) based on site purpose.
05
The Mason
PURPOSE
To create a wireframe of the site layout based on the Poet’s content flow decisions.
BENEFIT
Creates a true “Grid System” which offers limitless possibilities for how content can be laid out on a website.
06
The Stylist
PURPOSE
To define the styling (colors, fonts, decorations, spacing...) for each section in the layout.
BENEFIT
Makes each individual section beautiful and combines them all harmoniously.
07
The Curator
PURPOSE
To pair the optimal component and content items for each section of the grid.
BENEFIT
Content is displayed as the site owner intended by respecting how it was entered in the site editor.
08
The Plumber
PURPOSE
Create the HTML wrapper for the content. Inject Google Analytics, Facebook OpenGraph... etc.
BENEFIT
Gives site owners the ability to integrate with modern web services.
09
The Screener
PURPOSE
To simplify the code in preparation for displaying the site at various browser sizes.
BENEFIT
Reduces the time a user has to wait for sites to load and render in the browser.
